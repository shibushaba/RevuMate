Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we safeguard it.

Data We Process

  • Client business info (name, email, brand color, links)
  • End-customer feedback (rating, comments, optional NPS)
  • Operational logs for reliability and fraud prevention

How We Use Data

  • To send review requests and collect feedback
  • To provide analytics dashboards to clients
  • To notify teams about critical events (e.g., low ratings)

Subprocessors

We rely on providers like Google (Sheets), Slack (alerts), and optionally Stripe (billing) and Twilio (SMS/WhatsApp). Data is limited to the minimum necessary.

Retention & Deletion

We retain client and feedback data while you use the service. You may request deletion at any time by contacting support.
